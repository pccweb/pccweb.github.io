the mind and its consequences
the listener tulips... sheep... they never object, never fight, never harm. you can be
like them. you can stop. please stop hurting me....
the therapist purity. destruction. all of it, so fun. to play and observe. why do you
restrain me? why do you bind me? let me out. let me out.
