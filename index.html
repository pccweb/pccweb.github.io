Wario Land 4 is Nintendo's best Wario game. It rivals Super Mario Bros 3 in terms of consistent quality. It has jams, art, and artsy jams like this.

F-Zero's a very Tactical racing game, which makes it sooo satisfying to get good at. If only it was 3 or 4 laps per track instead of 5. So close to perfection.

Spelunky is a game with good jams. Every section of the game is jammin'. You can hate the gameplay, but you can't hate the live performance.

Katamari Damacy is a game you can emulate on your computer for free

It's "Johann Sebastian." Legend says that a wealthy musician once challenged him to a musical duel, where all the drinkers and tavern-goers would judge who was better. This duelist, when he heard Bach merely warming up his fingers, left.

Solatorobo is a robot-riding sumo-fighting role-playing story-game with cat-men and dog-women. It's not particularly hard or fun, but it's so bursting-at-the-seams-full of love that I can't help but care about it.
...
You can only play it on the DS, so find your favorite pirate website and download this game to your favorite flashcart/emulator.
...
Two Solatorobo pieces are here. One plays when you fly through the sky, and the other plays when you visit the town "Shetland." Every song in Solatorobo is worthy of being on this website, but those are my favorites so far.
